Mango Melona Soju Cocktail

Description

Looking for a creamy sweet cocktail that isn't too boozy? This Melona ice cream soju cocktail is perfect for you! Refreshing and tasty, you'll feel like you are on a beach enjoying an ice cream bar with a twist of booze.

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 4oz of Mango soju
  • 4oz of Sprite or 7up (Any light tasting soda or sparkling water works)
  • 1 mango Melona ice cream bar

Step by step

Preparing the cocktail

  1. Fill glass about halfway with ice.
  2. Pour in soju and soda of choice.
  3. Add in a whole melona bar and use it as a stir stick until the ice cream melts into the drink.
